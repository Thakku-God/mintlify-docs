_These rules outline the standards and expectations for all gangs and organized criminal groups on the server, ensuring fair, balanced, and enjoyable roleplay for everyone._

## 1. Gang Formation

To form a gang, you must follow the Mallu City guidelines. Unauthorized gangs may be removed and their members may face disciplinary action.

### Requirements to Form a Gang

- All gang members must be whitelisted on the server.
- All members must be **18 years of age or older**.
- After submitting your application, your gang will be monitored
- During the monitoring period, you must maintain **at least 10 active members during peak hours**.
- Once your gang is approved, all members must attend a meeting with **MAFIA**.
- Every member must read and understand the basic gang rules.
- After your gang is officially announced, you may begin participating in **turf wars, gang activities, and rivalries**.

### Gang Member Status

_A gang member is_ never considered an ordinary citizen while they are a member of an official gang.

Gang members are expected to follow all gang and server rules at all times.

Depending on the severity of a rule violation or the circumstances of a situation, a gang member may face **disciplinary action**, including **temporary suspension or permanent removal from the gang**, in addition to any server punishments imposed by the Admin Team.

### **2. Respect the Server Rules**

- All gangs and their members **must comply with the official Mallu City server rules** at all times.
- The use of **cheats, hacks, exploits, harassment, hate speech, or explicit/inappropriate content** is strictly prohibited.
- Every gang member is responsible for maintaining fair play, quality Roleplay, and respectful behavior.
- Violations of server rules may result in **warnings, suspensions, temporary bans, or permanent bans**.
- If a violation is committed on behalf of or with the involvement of a gang, **the entire gang may be held accountable** and may face disciplinary action, including the suspension or removal of gang privileges.

### **3. Gang Roleplay Integrity**

- Remain **in character** at all times during Roleplay.
- Gang Characters  are always considered Gang members and must roleplay accordingly. They are never treated as ordinary civilians while they are members of an official gang.
- **Metagaming** (using out-of-character information for in-character decisions) is strictly prohibited.
- Create believable, immersive storylines and avoid actions that break the quality of Roleplay.

### **4. Non-Excessive Violence**

- All acts of violence must have a **valid Roleplay purpose**.
- Random killings, deathmatching, or indiscriminate attacks without proper RP are strictly prohibited.
- Gang conflicts should revolve around meaningful rivalries, objectives, and story-driven Roleplay.

### **5. Gang Territories**

- Respect the established territories of other gangs.
- Do not enter or invade another gang's territory without a valid Roleplay reason.
- Territorial disputes should be resolved through **Roleplay, negotiations, or approved server events**.

### **6. Conflict Resolution**

- Gangs are encouraged to resolve disputes through **Roleplay, negotiations, diplomacy, or official gang meetings** whenever possible.
- Do not rely on constant shootouts or unnecessary violence to resolve every conflict.

### **7. Communication Rules**

- Use only **server-approved communication methods**, including in-game voice chat, text chat, and other approved systems.
- The use of external communication platforms (such as Discord calls or other voice applications) to gain an unfair advantage during Roleplay or gang situations is strictly prohibited.

### **8. Out-of-Character (OOC) Communication**

- OOC communication should be used **only** for:
  - Technical issues
  - Emergencies
  - Staff instructions
  - Necessary rule clarifications
- Excessive or unnecessary OOC communication during Roleplay is prohibited, as it disrupts immersion and the overall RP experience.

### **9. Gang & Citizen Interaction**

- **Gang vs. Citizen interactions must remain non-hostile at all times.**
- Gangs may **not** fire upon, threaten with weapons, kidnap, rob, or escalate to violence against citizens.
- Interactions between gangs and citizens are limited to **basic verbal Roleplay** unless otherwise permitted by specific server rules or staff-approved events.
- Citizens may **not** provoke, challenge, or intentionally bait gangs into hostile situations or combat.
- Gangs may **not** force citizens into violent Roleplay or into gang-related conflicts.
- Both gangs and citizens are expected to maintain **high-quality, realistic Roleplay** and treat each interaction as an opportunity to enhance the server's RP experience.

### **10. Police vs Gang**

**Police vs. Gang situations are completely prohibited.**

- Gangs may not initiate conflicts, raids, or wars against the **MCPD**.
- The **MCPD** may not participate in gang wars or gang-related conflicts unless required by normal law enforcement duties.
- Any attempt to organize or engage in a **Police vs. Gang** situation will be considered a rule violation and may result in staff action.

### **11. Gang Members & Vehicle Limits**

#### **Gang Member Limits**

- A minimum of **5 members** is required to participate in any official gang situation or to use gang assets.
- **Pre-War Phase:** Maximum **30 members** may participate.
- **Gang War:** Maximum **15 members** may participate in the situation.

#### **Vehicle Limits (Pre-War & General Situations)**

- A maximum of **8 gang vehicles** may be used when participating with **30 members**.
- The total vehicle limit includes **1 helicopter**.

#### **Gang War Vehicle Limits**

- Maximum **15 players** may participate.
- A maximum of **5 vehicles** and **1 helicopter** may be used.

#### **General Requirements**

- All gang situations must be conducted using **official gang attire** and **approved gang vehicles** only.
- **Civilian disguises are strictly prohibited.** Once you enter a gang situation, you must remain identifiable as a gang member at all times.

#### **Anti-VDM System (Server-Wide)**

- The server uses an **Anti-VDM System** to track VDM violations.
- **Three VDM violations** will result in an **automatic server kick**.
- VDM strikes are tracked across multiple incidents.

#### **What Counts as VDM**

The following actions are considered **Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)**, including but not limited to:

- Intentionally **pitting** a player's vehicle to force them to lose control or spin out.
- Intentionally **running over or killing** a player with a vehicle.
- Pitting a player and then intentionally running them over or killing them while they are down.

#### **VDM During a Gang War**

- If VDM occurs during an active gang war, immediately **notify all participants in Gang Chat** and **call for Shadows**.
- The gang war will be **temporarily paused** while **Shadows** review the available POV evidence.
- If Shadows confirm that VDM occurred:
  - The offending player will be **kicked from the server** and **may not rejoin the ongoing gang war**.
  - If a player was eliminated as a result of the confirmed VDM, they will be **revived** and may **rejoin the gang war**.

#### **Reporting & Enforcement**

- All VDM reports must include **clear, unedited POV footage**.
- Delayed, incomplete, or edited POV footage may be rejected.
- **Shadows decisions are final.**
- Repeated VDM offenses or failure to comply with a Shadow's decision may result in **additional disciplinary action**.

### **12. Gang House Safe Zone**

- Every **official gang house** is designated as a **Safe Zone**.
- Rival gangs are **not permitted to enter, provoke, or initiate any gang-related situation** within a gang house safe zone.
- Before taking any action against unauthorized players inside or around your gang house, you must issue **3 clear warnings** through **Gang Chat**.
- If the warned players respond by opening fire inside the Gang House Safe Zone, it will be treated as **Fail Roleplay (FRP)** and may result in staff action.
- Rival gangs **must not camp, wait, or remain directly in front of an opposing gang house or within its visible vicinity**.
- Rival gangs must provide the opposing gang with **reasonable space and a fair opportunity to leave their gang house** before any gang situation can begin.
- Blocking exits, surrounding a gang house, or preventing gang members from leaving the safe zone to start a situation is strictly prohibited and will be treated as **Fail Roleplay (FRP)**.

### **13. Gang House Breaching**

- **Every official gang house is designated as a Safe Zone.**
- **Gang house breaching is strictly prohibited under all circumstances.**
- No gang may enter, raid, attack, or force entry into another gang's house for any reason.
- Any attempt to organize or participate in a gang house breach will be considered a **serious rule violation** and may result in disciplinary action by the Admin Team.

### **14. Crash & Death Rules**

#### **Game Crash**

- If you crash during an active gang situation, you must **spawn at your last known location** and may rejoin the situation if permitted under the server rules.
- After reconnecting, **changing your last spawn location or intentionally spawning at a different location is considered Fail Roleplay (FRP)**.
- Players found abusing the spawn system by changing their spawn location will be subject to **admin action**, including an **inventory wipe**, and the incident will be treated as **FRP**.

#### **Death During a Gang Situation**

- If **Gang A burns the body of a member from Gang B**, the eliminated player will **lose their inventory** upon respawning.
- After respawning from a burned body, the player will retain **only**:
  - Cash
  - Black Money
  - Phone
- All other inventory items will be permanently removed.
- Burning a body is only valid if it complies with the server's gang rules and Roleplay standards.

### **15. Hostage Rules**

- A **gang member may only be taken hostage** if **at least 5 active members of their gang are currently in the city**.
- The minimum requirement of **5 active gang members** must be maintained throughout the hostage situation. Excuses such as _"There were 5 members at the start, but some left later"_ will not be accepted.
- During an **active gang situation**, you may **not** take a gang member hostage after they have been downed or killed.
- **Hostages may not be held for longer than 30 minutes** under any circumstances.
- **Gang Leaders and Co-Leaders may not be taken hostage.**
- If a Gang Leader or Co-Leader is mistakenly taken hostage, they **must be immediately replaced by another eligible gang member**.
- **The Gang Leader or Co-Leader is not required to participate in negotiations or make any demands** while being replaced.
- Until the replacement is completed, the Leader or Co-Leader **must continue to roleplay as a genuine hostage**, showing appropriate fear and complying with the hostage scenario. They may not use their protected status to gain an unfair advantage or disrupt the Roleplay.

### **16. Gang House Situation Restrictions**

- Once a gang situation has been initiated, **you may not retreat to, camp, or continue the situation inside your own gang house**.
- **Do not start, continue, or hold any gang situation in front of a gang house**, including entrances, exits, or the immediate surrounding area.
- **Do not hold defensive positions or camp near an opposing gang's gang house** to gain an unfair advantage or prevent them from leaving.

### **Admin Involvement Policy**

- Players may **not request, demand, or pressure Admins** to take immediate action during ongoing RP or PvP scenarios.
- Admin assistance may only be requested if you are **unable to continue playing due to a bug, glitch, or other technical issue**.
- **Do not call for Admins during any gang situation.**
- If you are unsure about a rule, **refer to the server rulebook** instead of interrupting Admins during an active situation.
- During gang situations, **Admins will not intervene in Fail Roleplay (FRP) incidents**. Continue the situation and submit an **FRP ticket** after it has concluded.
- If a situation becomes **uncontrollable, severely disruptive, or threatens server stability**, Admins reserve the **full authority to intervene and make a final decision**. Their decision is final and must be respected.

### 17. POV Requirements

- All participants involved in a situation **must maintain a continuous, unedited, and unmuted POV recording**.
- Every POV must clearly capture the following:
  - The **escalation** leading to the situation.
  - All relevant **communication** (voice and/or text).
  - The complete **outcome** of the situation.
- Edited, cut, incomplete, muted, or corrupted POV footage may be **rejected** and a decision may be made against the player if sufficient evidence is not provided.
- Players are responsible for ensuring their POV is clear and available upon request.
- For **PvP situations**, all participants must comply with the server's **PvP Acknowledgement** requirement in addition to these POV rules.
