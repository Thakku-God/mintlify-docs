---
title: "Second Character Rules"
icon: "user-group"
---

To maintain fairness, realism, and high-quality roleplay, all players must follow the rules below when creating and using a second character.

## Approval Requirement

- A second character may only be created if your primary (first) character is a member of a gang and you wish to participate in Police Department (PD) or EMS roleplay.
- In such cases, you are permitted to create a second character specifically for PD or EMS roleplay purposes, and vice versa.

---

## No Character Linking

- Your main and second character must not have any connection.
- No sharing of:
  - Money
  - Vehicles
  - Properties
  - Weapons
  - Businesses
  - PD or EMS job items
  - PD confiscated items
  - Contacts
  - Any other assets or resources
- Both characters must be completely separate individuals with no storyline overlap.

---

## No Metagaming

- Information learned on one character cannot be used on another character.
- This includes:
  - Criminal activity
  - Police investigations
  - Gang information
  - PD or EMS information
  - Player identities
  - Any other roleplay knowledge
- Any transfer of information between characters will be treated as metagaming and will result in strict punishment.

---

## No Rule Circumvention

- A second character cannot be used to:
  - Avoid punishments
  - Escape roleplay consequences
  - Bypass bans or jail time
  - Rejoin situations involving your primary character
- Violating this rule may result in the removal of both characters.

---

## Character Switching & Revenge RP Policy

- Triggering a situation while playing your PD character and then switching to your gang character to continue, influence, or benefit from the same situation is strictly prohibited.
- Any player found doing so will have their PD character removed without prior warning.
- If you are involved in a conflict while playing your gang character, you are prohibited from switching to your PD character to take revenge or interfere in that situation.
- Such actions will be considered Revenge RP and metagaming and will result in disciplinary action.

---

## Roleplay Quality

- Both characters must have:
  - Realistic backstories
  - Different personalities
  - Different lifestyles
- Creating a second character solely for financial gain or economic advantage is not permitted.

---

## Abuse of Economy

- Transferring assets between characters, whether directly or indirectly through friends or third parties, is strictly prohibited.
- Any attempt to manipulate the server economy using multiple characters will result in severe punishment.

---

## Punishments

Violations of the Second Character Rules may result in:

- Character wipe
- Suspension of second character access
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban (for severe or repeated violations)
