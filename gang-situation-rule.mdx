_These rules define how gang-related roleplay situations, conflicts, and engagements must be conducted to ensure fair, balanced, and roleplay-focused gameplay._

### **2. One Situation at a Time**

- A gang may only participate in **one active situation** at any given time.
- Joining or initiating another situation before the current one has ended is strictly prohibited.

### **3. Third-Party Interference**

- Third-party interference (3rd partying) is **strictly prohibited**.
- If a gang situation is already in progress, all unrelated players and gangs must stay away unless they are directly involved.

### **4. Safe Zone Restrictions**

Gang-related activities may **not** be started, continued, or concluded within any **Safe Zone**.

Safe Zones include, but are not limited to:

- Hospitals
- Job Locations
- Legal Businesses
- Police Properties
- Event Areas
- Gang Houses (including your own)

### **5. Gang Cooldown Rules**

- After a **main gang situation**, both gangs are subject to a **3-day cooldown**.
- After a gang war against a specific gang, you **cannot declare another war against the same gang for 3 days**.
- After completing a gang war, you must wait **24 hours** before declaring a war against a **different gang**.

### **6. Gang Situation Rules**

- **No gang situations may be initiated after 1:00 AM.**
- **All gang wars must conclude within 2 hours.** If the time limit is reached, the **gang with the most surviving members** will be declared the winner.
- If **Fail Roleplay (FRP)** occurs during a situation, **do not stop the ongoing scenario**. Continue the situation and report the FRP Ticket according to the FRP Ticket rules . **Shadows** may intervene immediately only in cases of **major FRP**.
- **Rooftop camping is permitted for a maximum of 5 minutes** during an active gang situation. Remaining on a rooftop beyond this limit is prohibited.

### **7. Gang War Structure**

Gang wars consist of the following official phases:

- **Initial Situation**
- **Pre-War Phase** _(optional)_
- **Final War Phase**

**Initiation is not considered an official phase** of a gang war.

**City-side gang wars and city-side pre-war situations are strictly prohibited.**
