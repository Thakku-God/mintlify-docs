_Streamers play an important role in growing the Mallu City community by showcasing high-quality roleplay and creating engaging content. To maintain a positive experience for everyone, all streamers must follow the rules below._

### 1. Voice Channels & Discord Discussions

- When you are in a Mallu City Discord voice channel or any staff/community discussion (excluding public community meetings), you must mute your Discord audio on stream.
- If you are roleplaying while connected to a Discord voice channel, keep yourself **Discord-muted** and never use information obtained through Discord during roleplay.
- Using Discord information in roleplay is considered **Metagaming** and will be punished accordingly.

### 2. Staff Discussions & Rule Discussions

- If you are involved in a Fail RP, staff, or rule discussion, you must either:
  - Mute your livestream, or end your stream until the discussion has concluded.
- Recording, streaming, or uploading clips of private staff discussions without permission is strictly prohibited.
- Management reserves the right to take appropriate action for any violation of this rule.

### 3. Server & Community Respect

- Streamers are expected to represent Mallu City professionally.
- Constructive criticism is always welcome; however, publicly degrading the server, staff, or community with the intent to create negativity is prohibited.
- Avoid insulting, harassing, or targeting other players on stream.
- Remember that your audience may become future members of the community, so always promote a positive environment.

### 4. Streamer Role Requirements

To be eligible for the **Streamer** role, you must:

- Stream **Mallu City** consistently for at least **7 days**.
- Include the official **Mallu City Discord invite link** in your stream description.
- Use the official server tags provided by the Management Team.
- Produce consistent, high-quality roleplay content while following all server rules.
- Submit a **Support Ticket** with proof of your streams for verification.

The **Streamer Role** is subject to periodic reviews to ensure it is being used appropriately.

### 5. Streamer Responsibilities

- Follow all Mallu City server rules while streaming.
- Do not exploit your Streamer role for personal gain or special treatment.
- Do not encourage rule breaks, toxicity, or harassment.
- Always represent the server professionally while live.

### 6. Streamer Perks & Role Reviews

- Streamer perks and benefits may change at any time based on Management decisions.
- The Management Team reserves the right to suspend or revoke the Streamer role if you violate server rules or these guidelines.
- If your Streamer role is revoked, you must wait **30 days** before submitting a new application unless otherwise decided by Management.
