## 1. Club Formation

1. Every Club must have a unique and appropriate name approved by Management.
2. A Club must consist of a minimum of **10 members** and a maximum of **15 members**, including the Club Manager, Lawyer, and all registered members.
3. Only officially registered Club members may participate in Club operations, activities, or represent the Club in roleplay.
4. To become an Official Club within MCRP, the Club must satisfy all eligibility requirements established by Server Management.
5. Once Management grants preliminary approval, the Club must meet with **MC BROS** to complete the final approval process, including all required roleplay procedures such as obtaining a Business License and completing all legal documentation under MC BROS.
6. Clubs intending to participate in any Business Auction must obtain prior approval from **MC BROS** before the auction begins.
7. Players must have the required minimum in-game playtime of **--** before becoming eligible to join a Club.
8. A player leaving a Club to join a Gang will be subject to a **one-week cooldown period**.
9. During the cooldown period:
   - The player may not join any other Club.
   - All Club-related roles and permissions will be removed.
   - The cooldown applies regardless of whether the player leaves voluntarily or is removed.
10. When a member leaves or is removed from a Club, the vacant membership slot may not be filled until the one-week cooldown period has expired.

## 2. Club Operations

1. Clubs are responsible for operating realistic and sustainable businesses. Server Management does not guarantee profitability or roleplay opportunities for any Business.
2. Clubs are strictly prohibited from initiating, participating in, or supporting criminal conflicts, Gang Wars, or violent situations. Any member violating this rule may be permanently removed from the Club and punished according to server rules.
3. All Club Business premises are designated as **Hard Safe Zones**. No roleplay situations, conflicts, pursuits, robberies, or combat may be initiated, continued, or moved into these locations.
4. Club members must wear their approved Club uniform while on duty or performing official Club duties.
5. A Club that remains inactive for more than **one week** after receiving its Business may be declared inactive by Management. The Club may be dissolved and its Business may be reclaimed for future auction or purchase.
6. Only registered Club members are permitted to work for or represent the Club in any official capacity.
7. Clubs have the right to refuse service or deny access to individuals within roleplay, provided there is a valid roleplay-based reason.

## 3. Club Assets

1. All Club assets remain exclusive to the Club and may not be transferred, shared, traded, or loaned to other Clubs or non-members.
2. Club vehicles are to be used solely for legitimate Club Business operations.
3. Club vehicles may not be used during criminal situations, pursuits, robberies, or any other roleplay scenarios unless explicitly authorized by Management.
4. Club communication channels are intended exclusively for Club-related Business and operational discussions.
5. Club assets may only be used by officially registered Club members.

# Business Rules

1. All purchases, transfers, leases, ownership changes, and hiring related to Businesses must be processed exclusively through **MC BROS**. Any unauthorized ownership transfer is strictly prohibited.
2. Every newly formed Club must pay a **Government Registration Fee of ₹5 Crore**. This amount will be deducted from the final auction payment made to **MC BROS** upon successful Business acquisition.
3. Business owners are responsible for ensuring their Business remains active, properly managed, and compliant with all server regulations.
4. Businesses must operate in a realistic manner and must not be used to exploit game mechanics or generate unfair advantages.
5. Any Business found inactive for more than **one week** may be reclaimed by Management and placed back into auction or made available for purchase.
6. Any owner found violating these regulations may be subject to one or more of the following disciplinary actions:
   - Immediate revocation of Business ownership.
   - Monetary fines determined by Management.
   - Suspension or permanent removal of Business privileges.
   - Additional disciplinary action where deemed appropriate by Server Management.

# Business Proposal Policy

1. All new Business proposals and ideas must be submitted directly to **MC BROS**.
2. **MC BROS** will review the proposal and forward it to the Government for official consideration.
3. The Government holds the sole authority to approve, reject, modify, or request additional information regarding any proposed Business.
4. All further procedures, licensing requirements, approvals, and implementation processes will be determined and communicated by the Government through **MC BROS**.

# Final Authority

1. The **Government** retains the final authority over all matters relating to Club registration, Business ownership, approvals, transfers, auctions, disputes, and rule enforcement.
2. Management reserves the right to amend these rules whenever necessary to maintain fairness, realism, and server stability.
