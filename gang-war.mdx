_These rules outline the procedures, restrictions, and expectations for declaring, conducting, and concluding official gang wars._

#### **1. War Declaration**

- A **Gang War** must be declared **face-to-face** during an official gang meeting between the gang leaders or their authorized representatives.
- Gang leaders are responsible for ensuring that all participating members are informed about **which gang they are officially at war with**.
- The **war declaration** and the **first attack** must be **two separate Roleplay scenes**. Attacking immediately after declaring war is not permitted.

#### **2. War Location**

- The Gang War location must be **mutually agreed upon during the gang meeting**.
- Only **one** of the following official war zones may be selected:
  - Sandy Shores
  - Paleto Bay
  - Harmony

#### **3. Death & Respawning**

- Players who are eliminated during a Gang War **must immediately respawn** using the server's revive system.
- Failure to respawn without a valid reason may result in the player **being barred from participating in gang situations for up to 5 days**, or for a duration determined by the Shadows/Admin Team.

#### **4. Ending a Gang War**

- A Gang War must also be **officially concluded through a face-to-face meeting** between both gangs.
- In most situations, a genuine **request for peace should be accepted**, unless there is a valid and significant Roleplay reason to continue the war.

#### **5. Cooldown**

- After a **Gang War** is concluded, both gangs are subject to a **3-day cooldown**.
- During this cooldown period, the **same two gangs may not initiate another gang situation, gang war, or breach against each other**.
