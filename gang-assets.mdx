## Free Gang Assets

Every registered gang will receive the following **free server-provided assets**:

- Gang Jacket
- Common Gang Vehicle
- Gang House

These assets are provided by the server and must follow the optimization requirements below.

### Texture Limits

### Gang Jacket

- Maximum texture resolution: **2048 × 2048 (2K)**.
- Higher resolution textures will not be accepted.

### Common Gang Vehicle

- Maximum vehicle texture resolution (YTD): **2048 × 2048 (2K)**.
- All textures must be optimized to ensure stable server performance.

### Gang House

- Maximum total resource size: **40 MB**.
- The MLO/IPL must be properly optimized and should not negatively impact server performance.

## Important Notes

1. Textures exceeding **2K (2048 × 2048)** will not be accepted.
2. Free Gang House resources exceeding **40 MB** will not be accepted.
3. All assets must be properly optimized and should not cause FPS drops or server performance issues.
4. Management reserves the right to request further optimization or reject any asset that does not meet server standards.

## Templates

#### Templates for both the Gang Jacket and the Common Gang Vehicle will be available in the Discord Gang Section.

Please use the official templates when creating your designs.
