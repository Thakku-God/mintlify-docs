# Free Gang Assets

Every registered gang will receive the following **free server-provided assets**:

- Gang Jacket
- Common Gang Vehicle

These assets are provided by the server and must follow the optimization requirements below.

## Texture Limits

### Gang Jacket

- Maximum texture resolution: **2048 × 2048 (2K)**
- Higher resolution textures will not be accepted.

### Common Gang Vehicle

- Maximum vehicle texture resolution (YTD): **2048 × 2048 (2K)**
- All textures must be optimized to ensure stable server performance.

## Important Notes

1. Textures exceeding **2K (2048 × 2048)** will be rejected.
2. Assets must remain optimized and should not cause unnecessary performance loss.
3. Management reserves the right to request further optimization if required.

### Templates

Templates for both the **Gang Jacket** and the **Common Gang Vehicle** will be provided in the **Discord Gang Section**.

Please use the official templates when creating your designs. Designs that do not follow the provided templates may be rejected or returned for revision.
