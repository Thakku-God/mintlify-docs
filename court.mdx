## Government Roleplay

### 1. Professional Conduct

- All government employees must maintain professional behavior while on duty.
- Abuse of government authority, powers, or privileges is strictly prohibited.

### 2. Abuse of Power

- Government officials may not misuse their position for personal gain, favoritism, revenge, or to benefit any gang or organization.

### 3. Corruption

- Corruption roleplay is only permitted with prior approval from server administration.
- Unauthorized corruption, bribery, or abuse of office is prohibited.

### 4. Government Confidentiality

- Government employees may not leak confidential information, investigations, warrants, or classified documents without valid roleplay.

### 5. Government Property

- Government vehicles, equipment, weapons, and facilities must only be used for official duties.

### 6. Resignation & Dismissal

- Government employees must follow server procedures when resigning or changing departments.
- Unauthorized removal of government equipment after leaving a department is prohibited.
