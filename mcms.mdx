_The Mallu City Medical Service (MCMS) provides emergency medical care and ensures realistic medical roleplay. All players must respect MCMS personnel and cooperate during medical scenarios._

## Interaction Rules

### 1. Respect MCMS

- EMS personnel are a neutral faction and must be treated with respect.
- Do not insult, troll, or intentionally disrupt MCMS roleplay.

### 2. Follow MCMS Instructions

- Cooperate with MCMS during treatment and transport.
- Follow medical instructions unless your character has a valid RP reason not to.

### 3. Do Not Interrupt Treatment

- Do not interfere while MCMS are treating a patient.
- Give MCMS enough space to perform medical roleplay.

### 4. Active Scenes

- Do not force MCMS into active gunfights or dangerous situations.
- Wait until the scene is secured before requesting medical assistance.
- Allow Police to secure the area before MCMS enters.

### 5. Fake Medical Calls

- Do not make fake or prank emergency calls.
- Calling MCMS without a legitimate RP reason may result in server punishment.

### 6. Protect EMS Neutrality

- Do not force MCMS to take sides in criminal or police situations.
- Do not use MCMS vehicles or personnel as cover during shootouts.

### 7. MCMS Vehicles

- Do not steal, damage, or block MCMS vehicles.
- Yield to ambulances responding with lights and sirens whenever possible.

### 8. During Treatment

- Do not walk away, drive off, or combat log while receiving medical treatment.
- Remain in roleplay until MCMS has completed treatment or transport.

### 9. Revive Expectations

- A revive is **not guaranteed**.
- MCMS will decide the appropriate medical treatment based on the roleplay scenario and server rules.
- Respect the outcome of the medical roleplay. \*MCMS will not examine or revive players who are being dead-carried.

### 10. Injuries

- Roleplay your injuries realistically.
- Do not instantly recover after receiving treatment if your injuries are severe.
- Continue to roleplay pain or limitations when appropriate.

### 11. Hospital Conduct

- Respect hospital staff and patients.
- No fighting, trolling, or causing disturbances inside the hospital unless it is part of an approved RP scenario.

### 12. No Abuse of MCMS Services

- Do not repeatedly call MCMS for non-emergency situations.
- Do not intentionally waste MCMS resources or time.

### 13. Kidnapping or Robbing MCMS

- EMS may not be kidnapped, robbed, or harmed in any RP reason .
- Random attacks on MCMS are strictly prohibited.

### 14. OOC Conduct

- Do not argue with MCMS staff over treatment decisions during RP.
- If you believe an issue occurred, complete the roleplay first and report it to server staff afterward.

### Failure to Follow These Rules

Failure to cooperate with MCMS, intentionally disrupting medical roleplay, or abusing MCMS services may result in disciplinary action from server side.
