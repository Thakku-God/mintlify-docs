# General Policies

## Refund Policy

1. Refund requests are accepted only within **5 days** of purchase with a valid reason.
2. No refunds will be issued after the refund period.

## Privacy Policy

1. Customer information will remain confidential.
2. Personal details will never be shared.

## Asset Collection

1. Assets must be collected within **5 days**.
2. If not collected, plan validity starts automatically on the **6th day**.

## Lifetime Plans

1. Lifetime plans remain valid until:
   - The server permanently closes.
   - Management decides to discontinue the plan.

## Underage Purchases

1. Users under 18 must obtain guardian permission before purchasing.
2. Refunds will not be provided due to missing guardian consent.

## Premium Validity

1. Donators will be notified before expiry.
2. Assets may be removed after expiry if not renewed.

## File Submission

1. Files must be submitted at least **24 hours** before restart.
2. Late submissions may be delayed until the next restart.
3. Corrupted or incomplete files will not be added.
4. Files must be submitted through a KeyMaster.

## General Conditions

1. Every asset is reviewed before being added.
2. Management may reject any asset affecting gameplay or server performance.
3. Repeated submission of non-compliant assets may result in suspension of donator privileges.
